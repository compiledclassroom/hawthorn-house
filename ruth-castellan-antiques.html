About

Ruth Castellan

Ruth Castellan has been acquiring and curating fine Victorian and Edwardian antiques in Foxglove Hollow for over twenty years. Before opening her shop in 2003, she spent a decade working with estate attorneys and property managers across Maryland, developing a specialty in the liquidation of inherited collections.

Her background in property and estate matters gives her a unique perspective that most antique dealers don't have. Ruth doesn't just value pieces — she understands the full picture of an estate and how to help families manage it efficiently.

"When a family inherits a property, they're often overwhelmed. My job is to make the antiques part of it completely painless — so they can focus on what actually matters."

Ruth is a lifelong Foxglove Hollow resident and a member of the Maryland Antique Dealers Association. She is available for confidential estate consultations throughout the county.
