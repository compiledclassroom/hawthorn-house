What Really Happened at Hawthorn House

DRAFT · NOT PUBLISHED · Written October 6th — three days before Eleanor's death

Every old house in Foxglove Hollow has a story nobody wants to confirm on the record. Hawthorn House has more than most. I've heard three different versions of what's supposedly hidden inside that place, and I intend to find out which one, if any, is true.

I have a feeling something is about to happen there. Call it a journalist's instinct. I just need to get inside.
