What We Do
Our Core Services

Our Philosophy
Discreet. Efficient. Institutional.

Meridian Land Group was founded on a simple principle: historic land holds more value than its current owners typically realize. Our job is to bridge that gap — efficiently, and on behalf of sellers who need to move.

We work exclusively with motivated sellers and their authorized representatives. We do not cold-call landowners or make unsolicited offers. We are introduced through attorneys, estate managers, and brokerage partners who know our process.

When Meridian is engaged, deals close. We have the capital, the legal infrastructure, and the institutional buyer relationships to move from introduction to close in 30 to 90 days.

  • Pre-funded acquisitions — no financing contingencies
  • Full discretion — we never publicize pending transactions
  • Title and encumbrance research handled in-house
  • Estate coordination team for inherited property situations
  • Compliant with all Maryland real property transfer requirements
Important Notice: Meridian Land Group operates as a private institutional investment firm. We work exclusively through authorized representatives and legal counsel. All inquiries regarding specific properties or pending transactions should be directed to our legal team. Meridian Land Group does not comment publicly on pending acquisitions.
